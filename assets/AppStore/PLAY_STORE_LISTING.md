## 14. ASO (App Store Optimization) Tips

1. **First 2-3 screenshots matter most** — show the AI voice wizard front and center (unique selling point)
2. **Use text overlays** on screenshots: "Plan in Tamil 🇮🇳", "Split Expenses 💰", "Works Offline 📡"
3. **Title keywords**: "AI" and "Trip Planner" rank high — keep them in
4. **Localize for India**: Add Tamil & Hindi store listings (Play Console → Custom store listings) — boosts regional discovery 3-4x
5. **Respond to every early review** — Play Store rewards engagement
6. **Update release notes with each version** — fresh content boosts ranking
