# Privacy Policy for Pathio

**Last Updated: November 10, 2025**

## Introduction

Pathio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Pathio mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, full name, and profile picture
- **User-Generated Content**: Trip details, checklist items, messages, and photos you share within the app
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers

### Location Information
- **When In Use**: We may collect your location when you use certain features, such as discovering nearby peers for offline messaging
- **Purpose**: Location data is used solely for enabling peer-to-peer connectivity and is not stored on our servers

### Usage Data
- **App Analytics**: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience
- **Crash Reports**: Diagnostic information is collected to identify and fix bugs

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Services**:
   - Create and manage your account
   - Enable trip planning and collaboration features
   - Facilitate group messaging and communication
   - Sync your data across devices

2. **Improve User Experience**:
   - Personalize app content and features
   - Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality
   - Debug and fix technical issues

3. **Security and Safety**:
   - Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
   - Enforce our Terms of Service
   - Respond to legal requirements

## Data Storage and Security

### Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

- **Supabase**: Backend database and authentication
  - Data stored in secure, encrypted databases
  - Location: Cloud servers (specify region if known)

- **Firebase Cloud Messaging**: Push notifications
  - Used only to deliver trip updates and messages
  - No message content is stored by Firebase

### Security Measures

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
- **Authentication**: Secure login using industry-standard protocols
- **Access Control**: Your data is only accessible to you and trip members you've invited

### Local Storage

- **Offline Data**: Trip and message data is stored locally on your device for offline access
- **Bluetooth/WiFi Direct**: Peer-to-peer messages are transmitted directly between devices without server storage

## Data Sharing

We **do not**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your information for marketing purposes without consent

We **may share** your information:
- With trip members you've invited (trip details, messages, photos)
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights, property, or safety

## Permissions

### iOS Permissions

Pathio requests the following permissions:

- **Camera**: To take profile pictures and trip photos
- **Photo Library**: To upload and save images
- **Bluetooth**: For offline peer-to-peer messaging
- **Local Network**: For WiFi Direct messaging
- **Notifications**: For trip updates and messages
- **Location (When In Use)**: For Bluetooth peer discovery

All permissions are optional and you can manage them in your device settings.
