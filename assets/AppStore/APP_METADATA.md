## App Description

### Full Description

```
Pathio - Your Ultimate Group Travel Companion

Plan amazing trips with friends and family, all in one place. From the Sanskrit "पथ" (path), Pathio helps you navigate every journey together.

🌟 KEY FEATURES

✈️ GROUP TRIP PLANNING
Create and manage trips with friends and family. Keep everyone on the same page from planning to departure.

✅ COLLABORATIVE CHECKLISTS
Build shared packing lists, to-do items, and travel tasks. Check off items together in real-time.

💬 REAL-TIME MESSAGING
Chat with your travel group online. When offline, continue messaging via Bluetooth and WiFi Direct peer-to-peer connectivity.

📸 PHOTO SHARING
Capture and share memories with your travel companions. Keep all trip photos in one place.

🔔 PUSH NOTIFICATIONS
Stay updated with trip changes, new messages, and checklist updates. Never miss important travel information.

🎨 BEAUTIFUL THEMES
Choose from 7 gorgeous themes to personalize your experience. Find the perfect look for your travel planning.

🔒 SECURE & PRIVATE
Your data is encrypted and secure. We respect your privacy and never sell your information.

🌐 OFFLINE CAPABLE
Continue planning and messaging even without internet. Perfect for international travel or remote destinations.

WHY PATHIO?

Planning group trips shouldn't be chaotic. Pathio brings together everything you need:
• Centralized trip information
• Easy coordination with travel buddies
• Offline functionality for anywhere, anytime access
• Simple, intuitive interface
• No ads, no spam, just pure travel planning

Whether you're organizing a weekend getaway, family vacation, or month-long adventure, Pathio keeps your group organized and connected.

📍 FROM PATH TO DESTINATION

The name "Pathio" comes from the Sanskrit word "पथ" (patha) meaning "path" or "journey." Every great trip starts with a path, and Pathio is your companion along the way.

Start planning your next adventure today with Pathio!

Download Pathio and transform the way you plan group travel!
```
