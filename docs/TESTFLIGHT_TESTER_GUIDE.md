# How to Test Pathio via TestFlight - Super Simple Guide

**Time needed:** 5 minutes (one-time setup)

**What you need:** Just your iPhone (that's it!)

---

## ✅ Don't Worry!

**Common Concerns (All False!):**

❌ "Do I need to create a new Apple ID?" → **NO!** Use your existing one
❌ "Will I need to logout of my iPhone?" → **NO!** Everything stays the same
❌ "Will it access my personal data?" → **NO!** TestFlight is just an app installer
❌ "Is it complicated?" → **NO!** Just 5 simple taps

**Bottom line:** You're just installing an app, like installing any app from the App Store!

---

## 📧 Step 1: Check Your Email

You'll receive an email with subject:
```
"You're invited to test Pathio"
```

**Open this email on your iPhone** (important - must be on iPhone, not computer)

---

## 📱 Step 2: Download TestFlight (One-time, 2 minutes)

### In the email:

Tap the blue button: **"View in TestFlight"**

**If you DON'T have TestFlight yet:**
- App Store will open
- Shows "TestFlight" app (made by Apple)
- Tap **"GET"** → **"INSTALL"**
- Wait for download (1-2 minutes)
- TestFlight installs on your home screen

**If you already have TestFlight:**
- TestFlight app opens directly
- Skip to Step 3

## ❓ Common Questions

### "Which Apple ID do I use?"
**A:** The one you're already logged in with on your iPhone. Check Settings → [Your Name] at the top.

### "Do I need to pay?"
**A:** NO! TestFlight is completely free. You're testing, not buying.

### "Will this affect my regular apps?"
**A:** NO! TestFlight apps are separate. Your regular App Store apps work normally.

### "Can I delete it later?"
**A:** YES! Just long-press the Pathio icon → "Remove App" (like any app)

### "Is my data safe?"
**A:** YES! Pathio works like the real app. Your data is stored securely.

### "What's the orange dot on the icon?"
**A:** That shows it's a TestFlight (beta) app, not the final App Store version.

### "Can I use it on my iPad too?"
**A:** If you have iPad, repeat these steps there. Same process!

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "I didn't get the email"
- Check spam/junk folder
- Make sure I have your correct email
- Wait 5-10 minutes (sometimes delayed)

### "TestFlight says 'No Apps Available'"
- Make sure you opened the email on your iPhone (not computer)
- Try tapping the email link again
- Contact me for a new invitation

### "Pathio won't install"
- Check iPhone storage (Settings → General → iPhone Storage)
- Need at least 200 MB free
- Restart iPhone and try again

### "App crashed or has bugs"
- That's why we're testing! Please report it to me
- Screenshot the error if possible
- Send me details: what you did before it crashed
