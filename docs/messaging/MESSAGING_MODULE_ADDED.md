## 🎯 Key Features Included

### Internet-Based Messaging
- ✅ Real-time messaging via Supabase Realtime
- ✅ Offline message queue with auto-sync
- ✅ Image & location sharing
- ✅ Push notifications & read receipts
- ✅ Emoji reactions & threaded replies

### Offline P2P Messaging (Your Key Requirement!)
- ✅ **Bluetooth LE P2P** - Device-to-device messaging
  - Range: 10-30 meters
  - Speed: ~1KB/s (text messages)
  - Battery: < 5% per hour

- ✅ **WiFi Direct P2P** - High-bandwidth messaging
  - Range: 50-100 meters
  - Speed: ~5MB/s (images, files)
  - Battery: < 8% per hour

- ✅ **Mesh Networking** - Relay through intermediate devices
- ✅ **Intelligent Sync** - Priority: Internet > WiFi > Bluetooth
- ✅ **Automatic Deduplication** - No duplicate messages
