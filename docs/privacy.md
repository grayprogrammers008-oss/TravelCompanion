# Privacy Policy for Pathio

**Effective Date:** May 16, 2026
**Last Updated:** May 16, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Pathio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the Pathio mobile application ("the App") on Android and iOS.

By using Pathio, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create a Pathio account, we collect:
- **Email address** (for authentication and account recovery)
- **Full name** (to display to your trip members)
- **Profile picture** (optional — uploaded by you)
- **Phone number** (optional — for trip member invites)

### 2.2 Trip & Content Data
- **Trip details** you create: name, destination, dates, budget, description
- **Itinerary & checklist items** you add
- **Expenses** you record (amounts, payers, splits)
- **Photos** you upload to trips
- **Messages** you send to trip members

### 2.3 Voice Data (AI Trip Wizard)
- **Spoken trip descriptions** processed by the device's native speech recognition
- Voice is processed **on your device** by Android Speech Recognizer or iOS Speech Framework
- We support **English, Tamil (தமிழ்), and Hindi (हिंदी)**
- Voice recordings are **not stored** by us or transmitted to our servers as audio — only the transcribed text is sent to our AI service to generate trip suggestions

### 2.4 Location Information
- **Approximate location** (when in use only): used to discover nearby peers for offline messaging via Bluetooth / WiFi Direct
- **No background tracking** — location is only accessed when you explicitly use offline messaging features
- Location data is **not stored on our servers**

### 2.5 Device Information
- Device model, OS version, app version (for crash reporting and compatibility)
- Anonymous device identifier (for push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging)

### 2.6 Usage Analytics
- Anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., features used, screen views)
- Crash reports and error logs (no personally identifiable content)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide the service** — create accounts, sync trips across devices, deliver messages
2. **Enable collaboration** — share your trip data with members you've invited
3. **Send notifications** — trip updates, new messages, checklist changes
4. **Improve the app** — fix bugs, analyze usage patterns, build new features
5. **Ensure security** — detect fraud, prevent abuse, enforce our Terms of Service
6. **Comply with law** — respond to legal requests, court orders, government requirements

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following service providers. Each handles your data according to their own privacy policies:

| Service | Purpose | Data Handled |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| **Supabase** | Backend, database, authentication | Account info, trips, messages, photos |
| **Firebase Cloud Messaging** (Google) | Push notifications | Anonymous device token, notification content |
| **Firebase Crashlytics** (Google) | Crash reporting | Anonymous crash logs, device info |
| **Google ML Kit** | On-device text recognition (bill scanning) | Image processed on-device, not transmitted |
| **Apple Speech Framework / Android Speech Recognizer** | On-device voice recognition | Voice processed on-device only |

Links to their policies:
- Supabase: https://supabase.com/privacy
- Firebase / Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy

## 5. Data Sharing

### We DO share:
- **With trip members you invite** — they can see trip details, your name, photo, and messages within that trip
- **With service providers** listed above, strictly to operate the app
- **When required by law** — court orders, subpoenas, legal process
- **In an aggregated, anonymous form** for analytics

### We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information to anyone
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your trips, messages, or photos for marketing
- Read your private messages between trip members
- Share your data with social networks

## 6. Data Storage & Security

- **Encryption in transit**: All data uses HTTPS/TLS
- **Encryption at rest**: Database storage encrypted by Supabase
- **End-to-end encryption**: Offline P2P messages (Bluetooth/WiFi Direct) are end-to-end encrypted
- **Access controls**: Only you and the trip members you invite can access your trip data
- **Hosting region**: Data is stored on Supabase servers (typically AWS regions in Mumbai/Singapore/US)

**No system is 100% secure.** We continuously work to improve, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Access** your data | Settings → Account → Export Data |
| **Correct** inaccurate data | Settings → Profile → Edit |
| **Delete** your account | Settings → Account → Delete Account |
| **Export** in a portable format | Settings → Account → Export Data |
| **Withdraw consent** | Uninstall app and request deletion via email |
| **Opt-out of notifications** | Device Settings → Pathio → Notifications |
| **Restrict processing** | Email us with details |

For India users — your rights under the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)** are honored.
For EU users — your rights under **GDPR** apply.
For California users — your rights under **CCPA** apply.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Pathio is not intended for users under **13 years of age** (under 16 in EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will delete it.

## 10. Permissions Used

### Android Permissions
- **Internet & Network State** — to sync with our servers
- **Camera** — to take profile pictures and trip photos *(optional)*
- **Storage / Photos** — to upload images *(optional)*
- **Microphone** — for AI Trip Wizard voice input *(optional, only when actively used)*
- **Location (when in use)** — for peer-to-peer offline messaging *(optional)*
- **Bluetooth & Nearby Devices** — for offline messaging *(optional)*
- **Notifications** — to deliver trip updates *(can be disabled)*

### iOS Permissions
- **Camera, Photos, Microphone, Bluetooth, Local Network, Location (When In Use), Notifications**
- All requested only when needed for a specific feature

You may revoke any permission anytime in your device's Settings.
