# Pathio Support

We're here to help. Here's how to reach us and find answers to common questions.

## Contact Us

- **Email**: [support@pathio.travel](mailto:support@pathio.travel)
- **Response time**: Within 48 business hours

For data protection or privacy concerns, please write to us with the subject line: **"Privacy Request — [your request type]"**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I create a trip?
Open Pathio, tap the **+** button on the Home screen, or use the **AI Trip Wizard** to speak your trip in English, Tamil, or Hindi. Add destination, dates, and members — your trip is ready.

### How do I invite friends to my trip?
Open the trip → tap **Members** → **Invite**. Share the invite link via WhatsApp, SMS, or email. Friends who tap the link will join the trip after creating a Pathio account.

### Can I use Pathio offline?
Yes! Once you've synced a trip, you can view, edit, and message offline. Pathio uses Bluetooth and WiFi Direct to send messages directly between phones — perfect for remote destinations.

### How does expense splitting work?
Add an expense in any trip → choose who paid and how to split (equal, custom, or percentage). Pathio calculates owed amounts automatically and shows everyone's balance.

### How do I change the voice recognition language?
Open the **AI Trip Wizard** → tap the language selector in the top-right → cycle between English, Tamil (தமிழ்), and Hindi (हिंदी).

### Is Pathio free?
Yes, Pathio is currently free with no ads. We may introduce premium features in the future, but core functionality will always remain free.

### How do I delete my account?
Settings → Account → **Delete Account**. All your data is permanently deleted within 30 days as outlined in our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/).

### What permissions does Pathio need?
We request permissions only for features that need them:

- **Camera / Photos** — For trip photos and profile pictures (optional)
- **Microphone** — For AI Trip Wizard voice input (optional)
- **Location** — For offline peer-to-peer messaging (optional, when in use only)
- **Bluetooth / Nearby Devices** — For offline messaging (optional)
- **Notifications** — For trip updates (can be disabled)

You can revoke any permission anytime in your device's Settings.

### My trip isn't syncing — what should I do?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Pull-to-refresh on the trip list
3. Sign out and sign back in (Settings → Account)
4. If the issue persists, email us at [support@pathio.travel](mailto:support@pathio.travel) with a screenshot

### How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
Email us at [support@pathio.travel](mailto:support@pathio.travel) with the subject line **"Bug Report"** or **"Feature Request"**.

Please include:
- Your device model and OS version
- Pathio app version (Settings → About)
- A clear description of the issue or idea
- Screenshots if applicable

**Grievance Officer**:
