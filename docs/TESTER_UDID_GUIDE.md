# How to Find Your iPhone UDID - Pathio Testing

Dear Tester,

Thank you for helping test Pathio! To install the app on your iPhone, I need your device's UDID (a unique identifier). Here's how to find it:

---

## Method 1: Using Mac (Easiest)

### Step 1: Connect Your iPhone
1. Connect your iPhone to your Mac using a USB cable
2. Unlock your iPhone
3. If asked, tap "Trust This Computer" on your iPhone

### Step 2: Open Finder
1. Open **Finder** (the blue smiley face icon in your dock)
2. Look at the left sidebar under "Locations"
3. Click on your **iPhone name**

### Step 3: Find Your UDID
1. You'll see your iPhone info screen
2. Click on the text below your iPhone name (shows things like "iOS 17.1", "64 GB", etc.)
3. **Keep clicking** until you see a long string of letters and numbers (like: `00008030-001234567890123A`)
4. That's your UDID!

### Step 4: Copy and Send
1. Right-click on the UDID → **Copy**
2. Send it to me via:
   - Email: [your email]
   - WhatsApp: [your number]
   - Message: "My UDID: [paste here]"

**What it looks like:**
```
Format: XXXXXXXX-XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX
Example: 00008030-001234567890123A
```

---

## Method 2: Using Windows (Alternative)

### Requirements
- iTunes installed ([Download here](https://www.apple.com/itunes/))

### Step 1: Connect Your iPhone
1. Connect your iPhone to Windows PC using USB cable
2. Unlock your iPhone
3. Tap "Trust This Computer" if asked

### Step 2: Open iTunes
1. Open **iTunes** application
2. Click the **iPhone icon** at the top left
3. Click on **Summary** in the left sidebar

### Step 3: Find Your UDID
1. Look for "Serial Number" section
2. **Click on "Serial Number"** text
3. It will change to show **UDID**
4. Right-click → **Copy**

### Step 4: Send to Me
Send the UDID via email or message.

---

## Method 3: Using iPhone Directly (iOS 16+)

### Step 1: Get Phone Info
1. Open **Settings** app on your iPhone
2. Tap **General**
3. Tap **About**

### Step 2: Find UDID (Hidden)
Unfortunately, UDID is not directly shown here. You'll need a Mac or Windows computer to see it.

1. Download **"UDID"** app from App Store (free)
2. Open the app
3. It shows your UDID on the screen
4. Tap **Copy** button
5. Send to me

**App Store Link:** Search "UDID" in App Store

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### What is UDID?
- UDID = Unique Device IDentifier
- It's like a serial number for your iPhone
- It's safe to share with me (I need it to allow app installation)
- It doesn't contain any personal information

### Privacy
- ✅ Safe to share
- ✅ Only used for app testing
- ✅ Cannot access your data
- ✅ Just allows me to add your device for testing

### Format Check
Your UDID should:
- ✅ Be 40 characters long (older devices) OR
- ✅ Be 25 characters with dashes like: `00008030-001234567890123A`
- ❌ NOT be your Serial Number (shorter, different format)
- ❌ NOT be your IMEI (15 digits)
