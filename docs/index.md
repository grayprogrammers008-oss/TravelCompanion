# Pathio

**Plan trips together — AI-powered, multilingual, group travel companion.**

Pathio is a mobile app that helps friends and families plan amazing trips together. Speak your trip idea in English, Tamil, or Hindi — and watch AI bring it to life. Split expenses, build collaborative checklists, message your group (online or offline), and keep every memory in one place.

## Features

- 🤖 **AI Trip Wizard** — Voice-driven trip creation in English, Tamil (தமிழ்), and Hindi (हिंदी)
- 👥 **Group Collaboration** — Real-time trip planning with friends and family
- 💰 **Smart Expense Splitting** — Track who paid, who owes, who's settled
- ✅ **Collaborative Checklists** — Shared packing lists, to-do items
- 💬 **Online + Offline Messaging** — Group chat works even without internet via Bluetooth and WiFi Direct
- 📸 **Trip Memories** — Photo galleries for every adventure
- 🎲 **Surprise Me!** — Random curated trip ideas across Indian and international destinations
- 🔒 **Privacy-First** — No ads, no tracking, end-to-end encrypted offline messages
