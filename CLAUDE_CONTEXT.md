   - All revenue reported in USD on Schedule C
   - Simple tax filing (no currency conversion calculations needed)

```
Indian customer pays: ₹999 INR
```

## 📖 Glossary of Terms

**L1B Visa:** Intracompany transferee visa for specialized knowledge workers. Cannot own business or receive business income in the US.

**L2 Visa:** Dependent visa for spouse of L1 visa holder. Can apply for EAD (work authorization).

**EAD:** Employment Authorization Document. Allows L2 visa holder to work and own business in the US.

**Schedule C:** IRS tax form for reporting profit or loss from business (sole proprietor).

**Sole Proprietorship:** Simplest business structure. Owner and business are same legal entity. Uses owner's SSN for taxes.

**SSN:** Social Security Number. Required for opening bank accounts and filing taxes.

**EIN:** Employer Identification Number. Optional for sole proprietors (can use SSN instead). Required for LLCs and corporations.

**USCIS:** United States Citizenship and Immigration Services. Government agency that oversees immigration.

**Spousal Gift:** Money given from one spouse to another. Tax-free with unlimited amount.

**Lazy Geocoding:** Optimization technique (used by WhatsApp) where GPS coordinates are only converted to addresses when user explicitly requests it. Saves 95%+ on geocoding API costs.

**Supabase:** Open-source Firebase alternative. Provides PostgreSQL database, authentication, real-time subscriptions, and storage.

**Riverpod:** State management solution for Flutter. Handles data flow between UI and business logic.

**StreamProvider:** Riverpod provider that emits continuous stream of data (used for real-time updates).

**RLS (Row Level Security):** PostgreSQL security feature that restricts which rows users can access based on policies.
